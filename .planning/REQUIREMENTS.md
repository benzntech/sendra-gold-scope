# Requirements

## v1 Requirements

### Authentication & Mobile App Client
- [ ] **APP-01**: User login requires Mobile Number and Referral ID.
- [ ] **APP-02**: Device authentication integration (OTP simulation or verification).
- [ ] **APP-03**: iOS & Android wrapper configuration (PWA manifests, mobile responsive layouts).

### Franchise & Branch Management
- [ ] **FRAN-01**: Map franchisee branches and assign unique identifiers.
- [ ] **FRAN-02**: Record and trace branch sales and business turnover.
- [ ] **FRAN-03**: Ledger for recording branch operational expenses.
- [ ] **FRAN-04**: Monthly revenue share calculator (1% fixed monthly share on ₹1 Cr deposit).

### Channel Partner (CP) Program
- [ ] **CP-01**: Promoter/Channel Partner portal registration with ₹1 Lakh security deposit record.
- [ ] **CP-02**: Referral network tracking mapping T1 (Direct Partner) and T2 (Referred Partner).
- [ ] **CP-03**: Tiered commission engine allocating 0.3% direct commission to T2, and 0.2% override commission to T1.
- [ ] **CP-04**: Monthly revenue benefit payout calculator (1% fixed return on ₹1 Lakh deposit).

### Old Gold Purchase
- [ ] **GOLD-01**: Old Gold Purchase entry portal with item weight, purity (Karat), and market valuation.
- [ ] **GOLD-02**: Checklist verification workflow for appraisers.
- [ ] **GOLD-03**: Receipt and purchase invoice generation for customers.

### Centralized Admin Dashboard
- [ ] **ADM-01**: Central control panel for admin to monitor real-time billing transactions.
- [ ] **ADM-02**: Management tables for branch mapping, expenses approval, and partner commission payouts.
- [ ] **ADM-03**: Old gold appraisal validation interface.

## Out of Scope
- **CHAT-01**: Real-time customer chat.
- **OATH-01**: OAuth login integrations (Google, Apple, Facebook) on storefront client app.
